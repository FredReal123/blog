How to Start a Garden at Home

Your Simple Beginner's Guide

1. Choose Your Garden Type

Decide if you want to grow vegetables, herbs, flowers, or a mix! Container gardening is great for small spaces.

2. Pick the Right Location

  • Sunlight: 6-8 hours daily
  • Good soil or potting mix
  • Easy access for care

3. Select Your Plants

Choose beginner-friendly plants like tomatoes, basil, mint, marigolds, or succulents based on your space and climate.

4. Prepare the Soil

Loosen soil, add compost, and clear weeds for healthy plant growth.

5. Plant & Care

  • Water regularly (but don’t overwater)
  • Use mulch to retain moisture
  • Watch for pests naturally

6. Enjoy & Harvest

Harvest herbs and veggies often. Take time to relax and appreciate your little green space!
